Penalty Shootout
Challenge

Choose your club, take penalties, defend the goal, and win the shootout.

How to Play

Set up your shot

Select a team, then start the match preview. Your kit colors come from the team you choose, and the opponent is selected automatically.

Use the three moving control bars at the bottom. Click the active bar or press Space to lock your choice.

Direction: controls left and right placement.

Height: controls low or high shot. Too high can miss the goal.

Power: controls shot speed. Too slow is easy to save; too powerful can go wide.

After all three values are locked, wait for the whistle. The player then runs up and kicks the ball.

Defend the goal

Click inside the goal to choose where your goalkeeper will dive.

A glove hint may briefly show the opponent shot target before you dive. The hint gets shorter as the tournament becomes harder.

Use SOUND: ON/OFF in the top-right corner to control crowd ambience and whistles.

Scoring: your goals give 100 points, goalkeeper saves give 50 points, and each match win gives a 500 point bonus.

Each side takes five penalties. If the match is tied after five rounds, it continues in Sudden Death.

Select Your Team

Your player kit will use the selected club colors. The opponent will be selected randomly.
